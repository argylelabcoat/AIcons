"desc/gr-misc"
Grey Scale Icons of miscellaneous size.

These icons are Grey Scale and as such often contain a large number of shades of grey in them. They are probably best used on a Grey Scale Display terminal.

At this time there is no specific sizes for grey icons, what you see is what you get :-( .
