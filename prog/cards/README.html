"prog/cards"
Sets of Playing Cards

In this directory are sets of playing cards plus 4 extra cards for the top, flip side, of the cards. Each set thus has 56 cards.

Most of these cards are monocrome bitmap images which will need to be colored appropriately. No one has contributed color X pixmap images of playing cards yet.
