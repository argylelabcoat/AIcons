Programming Icons. (sorted by type and use)
Icons which are generally used within actual applications.
NOTE: This section is also a catch all for special usage.

  • Small scale texture patterns. (mostly 16x16 bitmaps)
    See also "Background Titles" for larger patterns.
  • Monochrome Bits and Pieces of icons.
  • Color Bits and Pieces of icons.
  • Framing templates to wrap around various icons.
  • Fancy fonts to DIY messages with.
  • Icons that form an animation sequence of some sort.

  • Playing cards (in two sizes)
  • bitmaps for planview adventures. (16x16 pixels)
  • Space Ships, Tanks, etc 16 compass directions. (mono and color)
  • Missiles flying in 8 compass directions.
  • Explosions of various sorts
  • Various landscape and floor icons.
  • Icons to build walls, and fences with.
  • Building icons. (houses, castles, ...)
  • Miscellaneous objects and items. (swords, potions, gems, ...)
  • Devices and switches. (levers, switches, ...)
  • Beasts and monsters from crossfire.
  • Pattern icons that were used for magic effects.
