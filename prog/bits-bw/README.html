"prog/bits-bw"
Bits and Pieces of Icons (bitmaps)

The black and white bitmaps in this directory are NOT complete icons in themselves. They are small symbols, images and parts, which can be used to generate larger icons, symbols and displays, by mixing and matching.

Some parts can be fited together like a jigsaw puzzle by other programs or by hand, to build a larger more complicated structure. For example `z_pipes.xbm'.

See Also:
