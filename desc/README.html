Descriptive Icons. (sorted by size and type)
Icons for general use as Window Icons and Background Images.

std Standard 64x54 bitmap icons. (Good X window size)
std-cl Standard 64x54 color pixmap icons. (Good X window size)
std-fcl Standard 64x54 pixmaps all colors. (Good X window size)

bw-16 Black & White icons 16x16. (small symbols)
bw-32 Black & White icons that are all 32x32 in size. (Pc/Mac size)
bw-48 Black & White icons that are all 48x48 in size.
bw-64 Black & White icons that are all 64x64 in size.
bw-misc Black & White icons of miscellaneous size.
bw-bgnd Large background tiles. (IE: escher fish tile)
See also "Pattern Tiles" for very small background tiles.

cl-18 Grey with some color 18x18 pixels in size. (small symbols)
cl-20 Colorful icons 20x20 pixels in size. (small symbols)
cl-32 Color icons 32x32 pixels in size. (Pc/Mac size)
cl-48 Color icons 48x48 pixels in size.
cl-64 Color icons 64x64 pixels in size.
cl-64x38 Color icons in the `ctwm' pixmap size (64x38).
cl-misc Color icons of miscellaneous sizes.
cl-bgnd Color background tiles. (EG: for displays and documents)

gr-misc Grey Scale icons of miscellaneous size
gr-bgnd Grey Scale background tiles.
