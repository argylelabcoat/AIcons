"prog/beasts"
beasts and monsters

In the sub-directories are found beasts and other monsters or critters which could be encoundered in plan view adventures or many sorts. They are presented here as a source for descriptive icons to create and use.

NOTE: In Role playing games from which such plan view games originally derive a `monster' is any encountered person/beast. That is they could include other adventurers. As suchalso in here are a large number of `human' monsters.
