"prog/objects"
objects and items

In the sub-directories are found objects and items which could be encoundered in plan view adventures or many sorts. They are presented here as a source for descriptive icons to create and use.

NOTE: Some of the objects could be carried by and adventurer in a game, others are just found or present in a planview map as `decoration'. Other items are `transport' which could be entered by adventurers to go from place to place. These later do not class as `ships' in this collection as they are just an image without any `direction'.
